Among the forward-thinking firms that have decided to fully explore what the cognitive enterprise has to offer is Goldcorp.

At this North and South American-based gold producing company, geologists spend an estimated four-fifths of their time analyzing and preparing data from unstructured logs and models.

But when they used a data platform in the cloud created by IBM's Watson, they were able to create sophisticated calculations with machine learning models and cut down their analysis time of 80 years' worth of data from 165 to just 4.5 hours, which vastly freed up geologists to work more productively.
